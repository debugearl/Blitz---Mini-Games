About Us

We are a team of student developers creating fun and simple mini games.
Our goal is to learn, improve, and share enjoyable gaming experiences with everyone.
This project is our way of growing our skills while making something players can enjoy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fun and engaging mini-games that anyone can play directly in their browser. We aim to be a creative hub where student developers can grow their skills, turning simple game ideas into enjoyable, accessible experiences for a global audience.

Our Vision

We envision a platform where users can relax, challenge themselves, and enjoy games without downloading anything. Our long-term vision is to establish a respected portfolio of games, demonstrating the creative and technical potential of our student team to future employers and the wider community.
